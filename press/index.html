// Biography

SQUIGL is an independent artist and producer working at the intersection of sound design, electronic production, Downtempo, and dance driven beats. Guided by the space between texture and feeling, SQUIGL builds music that is intentional from the frequency up.

Every release is a statement. Every sound is chosen. This is music made for the headphones-on, eyes-closed moment; and for the ones who feel it before they understand it.

Currently independent, SQUIGL is available for production credits, collaborative features, licensing opportunities, and sync placements. Reach out to start the conversation.
